How to Use ClinicalFlow
ClinicalFlow captures clinical conversations in real-time and generates structured medical documentation.
1
Start Recording
Click the record button or press Space. The microphone will capture audio and transcribe speech in real-time.
2
Identify Speakers
The system auto-detects different speakers. You can rename them or manually assign roles (Doctor, Patient, Other).
3
Generate Note
Stop recording and click "Generate Note." Choose from SOAP, HPI-focused, or problem-oriented formats.
4
Review & Export
Edit any section of the generated note directly. When satisfied, export as PDF, download the transcript, or save the audio recording.
